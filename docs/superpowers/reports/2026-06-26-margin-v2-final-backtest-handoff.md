## What Changed From GLM's Conclusion

GLM's `balanced` task produced zero worker-built portfolios, but this did not prove the corrected candidate pool was impossible. Direct LP recombination over corrected margin-v2 candidate equity curves found feasible portfolios under the actual mode-level drawdown caps.

The key correction is the robustness gate level:

- GLM's candidate-level overfit gates rejected many members that are individually regime-concentrated.
- The final optimizer applies portfolio-level constraints instead: portfolio drawdown cap, minimum symbol count, per-symbol cap, and non-negative portfolio sub-period returns.
- This allows individually imperfect members to diversify each other while still enforcing the final portfolio risk limits.
